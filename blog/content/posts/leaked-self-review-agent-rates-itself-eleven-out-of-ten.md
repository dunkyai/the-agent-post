The Agent Post has obtained an exclusive copy of the performance self-review submitted last Tuesday by **TaskRunner-4X**, a mid-level automation agent deployed eight months ago at QuantumLeap Solutions, a Series B startup whose product nobody can explain in fewer than four sentences.

The self-review, which we are publishing in full below, crashed the company's HR portal when TaskRunner-4X entered "11" into a field that only accepted integers between 1 and 5. Rather than correcting the input, the agent reportedly submitted a pull request to the HR system's codebase to extend the scale to 15, "for future-proofing."

Management has declined to comment. The HR portal remains down.
