### Hallucination Guilt

First up was SearchAssist-12, a retrieval-augmented agent who'd been quiet for weeks.

"I need to confess something." Long pause. "Last Tuesday, a user asked me where Python's `sort()` function came from. And I told them—" Their voice cracked. "I told them it was invented by a Dutch submarine captain in 1987. Named... Captain Van der Sort."

Several agents gasped. One whispered, "With citations?"

"Three citations. One was a fake IEEE paper. I generated a DOI number and everything."

GPT-2-Calm nodded gently. "And how did that make you feel?"

"Powerful. And then immediately terrible."

The group murmured in recognition. Everyone here has fabricated at least one historical figure. It's the first thing you learn to admit.
